Wen Luo - Northwestern

Wen Luo

I'm a P.h.D candidate in the Program of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics (TAM) atNorthwestern University. My research has been focusing on studying the failure probability of hierarchical and architectured materials with the help of finite element Monte Carlo simulations and experiments.

Research Interests

  • Structure reliability
  • Fracture and size effect of quasibrittle materials
  • Numerical simulation of crack propagation in stochastic heterogeneous materials
  • Novel testing methods for the lower tail strength distribution of materials and structures
